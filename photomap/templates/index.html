Illustrate your experiences abroad for your family and friends


Before we found KEIKEN, we had a hard time reporting about our exchange semester:

You can zip your photos and write a long description via email, but often a lot of pictures needs explaining to the unfamiliar eye

You can also give a presentation when you come back, but this will only reach the people close to you

You can also post the photos on your favourite social network, but not everyone you want to reach will be member there

Therefore we called KEIKEN to life. We hope to provide you with the tools to give everyone of your friends a presentation they deserve.
